Chapter 60

In Silver Fox,

Sandra squinted, her face expressionless.

She emitted an imposing aura.

“Mr. Cooper.”

“Hmm.”

Wesley replied flatly, his tone calm.

“I was thinking, do you care more about the feeling of fighting side by side with it?”

She was referring to the racing car.

Wesley rested his head against the car window.

His handsome yet somewhat pale face was illuminated by the headlights outside, elegant and graceful.

The cars behind them crazily chased after her, trying to surround and intercept her.

Soon, they found themselves immersed in a sea of cars.

“My old friend naturally prefers to be my sword.”

Wesley’s tone was cold, with a hint of murderous intent.

Sandra smiled. “Alright!”

When she reached the Z-bend, she suddenly accelerated, raising the speed of the car to 330 km/h.

The car behind them crashed into another car.

With a loud explosion, flames soared into the sky.

Fortunately, there were emergency personnel on the hillside.

Sandra’s car sped forward, and those who tried to crash into it instead collided with the cliff.

They were all left behind.

Soon, Sandra’s car reached the sixteenth bend.

There were still three cars ahead of her.

She raised the speed of the car to 380 km/h.

The engine roared.

The friction between the tires and the ground caused sparks to fly continuously.

Both the drivers ahead of her and the spectators held their breaths.

Sandra passed Jagger.

Then, she passed John.

Finally, she closed in on Daniel.

Suddenly,

Daniel’s car started to move in an S-shape.

“So this is Daniel’s big stunt.”

“Yes, he has killed and injured many racers overseas.”

“It would be cruel if it were intentional.”

“No one wants to give up the prize.”

“Does Daniel really want Mr. Cooper’s car to crash?”

“Mr. Cooper’s car was assembled by himself, and many people covet it.”

“If Daniel offends the Cooper family…”

“If Mr. Cooper dies, the Cooper family…”

The Cooper family would fall into Debra’s hands.

The spectators clenched their fists and gritted their teeth.

Sandra and Wesley couldn’t lose to Daniel!

Soon, Sandra’s car reached the final bend.

This was the last chance to overtake.

Sandra’s eyes flashed with determination as she placed both hands on the steering wheel.

She pushed the accelerator to the max.

The car sped forward like a bullet from a gun!

While Daniel’s car was still moving in an S-shape, Sandra seized the opportunity and rushed forward.

They drove side by side.

Daniel’s car collided with the cliff, producing bursts of sparks.

He turned the wheel, aiming to collide with Sandra’s car.

The two speeding cars crashed with a loud bang, sending a deafening shockwave through the valley.

Sandra’s car shook violently.

It was almost pushed off the cliff.

But there was no panic on her face. She even braced herself for the second collision.

Soon, there was a third crash.

Wesley, sitting in the passenger seat, frowned slightly. He was indeed fragile and couldn’t withstand such impacts.

How pathetic.

Sandra noticed something was wrong with him.

Her eyes turned bloodshot.

She had to make it quick!

She quickly turned the wheel.

Everyone saw her car plunge off the cliff.

“Ah!”

Someone screamed in fear.

Everyone’s heart skipped a beat.

They all watched in silence.

The next moment!

The car, which should have fallen off the cliff, miraculously reappeared on the mountain road! And it overtook Daniel’s car!

“Wow!”

A wave of cheers erupted from the crowd.

“Awesome!”

“Is she really a village girl?”

“She’s the real daughter of the Hill family!”

“She must have been guided by Mr. Cooper!”

“She’s amazing. I want to be her disciple!”

“Look, Daniel is going crazy!”

After performing a drift, Sandra’s car had surpassed Daniel’s by a large margin.

If Daniel didn’t do something, he would definitely lose.

Just then,

Daniel stepped on the accelerator and rushed toward the side rear of Sandra’s car.

If he succeeded, Sandra’s car would fall off the cliff, and he would win!

Everyone thought Sandra was doomed!

However, her car miraculously climbed up the cliff.

And it raced along the cliff!

Daniel tried to surpass her,

But her car descended from the cliff and collided with Daniel’s car on the passenger side.

The powerful impact made it impossible for Daniel to control the wheel, and his car plunged down the mountain.

Sandra’s car, riding the momentum, drifted across the finish line.

She even completed a difficult drift maneuver!

The crowd erupted in cheers!

Daniel’s car fell off the cliff, but he jumped out at the critical moment and climbed back up.

He had no ranking!

John and Jagger crossed the finish line separately.

Daniel punched the rock in frustration, and blood instantly flowed down the rock. He glared toward Sandra’s car.

Having won the championship, Sandra slowly opened the car door and stepped out.

Murderous intent and resentment were evident on her beautiful face. As she walked, she untied her hair, which softly brushed past her shoulders. She was stunning.

Daniel’s eyes blazed with fury. He wished he could kill the woman who had stolen his championship.

However, as Sandra walked toward him, a sense of unease suddenly arose in his heart.

He froze in place.

When he finally reacted, Sandra had already stopped in front of him and kicked him without hesitation.

He plunged down the cliff again,

But he managed to grab onto a boulder.

“Serves him right!”

“Daniel should be banned from domestic races!”

“What he just did was attempted murder!”

Ignoring the discussions around her, Sandra walked back to her car and opened the passenger door.

Wesley slowly got out, his face still expressionless,

But there was a hint of tenderness in his eyes.

Jagger looked toward them, holding the medal.

Sandra had completed a miraculous racing stunt.

She couldn’t have just researched Chris.

She was either Chris’ direct disciple or… Chris herself!

He had longed to become Chris’ disciple!

But he had despised Sandra as a country bumpkin, believed that she was a murderer, and shunned and neglected her!

.
