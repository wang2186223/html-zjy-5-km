Chapter 23

Ethan’s mouth twitched.

“Mr. Cooper, you…” He reluctantly smiled. “You must be kidding me.”

Wesley opened the car door and got out. Sandra didn’t know what he was up to and naturally followed him out.

“Mr. Clark, if I remember correctly, my Panamera was modified by this factory, right?”

Ethan nodded.

Wesley used to love cars before he became a vegetable. He liked to buy luxury cars and customize them himself. The Cooper family had a factory specializing in car production, so they had been helping him with the modifications.

So, every car Wesley had was modified in this factory.

“Now, understand?” Wesley sneered. “I had a car accident yesterday!”

It sent a chill down Ethan’s spine. His face turned pale.

“Mr. Cooper…” Of course, he knew about this. And he was also aware that it was normal for the Cooper family to have internal conflicts. He had once fought alongside Wesley’s grandfather, Terence Cooper. He couldn’t take sides. So, he had always remained neutral.

“My car has always been modified by you, but I had the accident because there was something wrong with the car. Humph…”

Wesley’s eyes were deep and sharp. “Shouldn’t I deal with you?”

Under immense pressure, Ethan broke into a cold sweat. “Mr. Cooper, your car hasn’t been maintained for several years.”

Wesley retorted, “So, is that the reason the car went out of control?”

After a pause, he continued, “Can you convince yourself?”

Ethan wiped the sweat from his forehead. “Mr. Cooper, please give me some time. I will investigate the modifications made to the car at that time and provide you with a satisfactory answer.”

Wesley waved his hand. “No need. I remember all the details of that car at the time.”

Joey immediately stepped forward, took out the documents from the car, and handed them to Ethan.

Wesley continued speaking. “Back then, your son had just graduated and urgently needed a car to build his reputation…”

He didn’t finish his sentence, but everyone understood.

Ethan didn’t expect to be directly blamed like this. He felt both anxious and angry, his blood boiling in his chest and a throbbing headache.

“Mr. Cooper…” He sighed heavily. “What do you want me to do?”

Wesley got straight to the point. “It’s simple. Think carefully about who your real boss is.”

Ethan was astonished. His boss was the Cooper Group. But was it Hailey, Debra, or now Victor and Wesley? He suddenly realized that, for all these years, he had been wavering. He had become the type of person he used to despise the most—a fence-sitter.

Wesley looked at his watch. “You have five minutes left.”

Ethan snapped out of his daze. Wesley’s meaning was simple. If Ethan continued to be a fence-sitter, his son would go to jail because of that car. If he sincerely joined Wesley, this matter could be forgotten. However, Hailey and Debra’s power was not to be underestimated. Could Wesley, who had been a vegetable for many years, really turn the tide?

“Mr. Cooper.” Hearing Wesley’s countdown, Ethan panicked. Finally, he gritted his teeth and made up his mind. “Mr. Cooper, you are my boss. That has never changed.”

Wesley didn’t seem pleased. Instead, he pressed Ethan further. “Talk is cheap.”

Ethan took a deep breath and pointed toward the office building. “Sir Cooper entrusted me with this factory and gave me the authority to handle the seals and account books.” He stepped aside and led Wesley and Sandra into the office building.

Soon, they entered Ethan’s office. Ethan opened the safe and brought out the seals and other items. “Mr. Cooper, I will have these remade according to your instructions.”

Wesley glanced at Joey. Joey immediately took the items and arranged for someone to handle the handover.

Ethan asked tentatively, “Mr. Cooper, that car… my son…” Wesley remained calm. “That car has been examined and determined to have been intentionally tampered with.”

After a pause, he stood up and concluded with determination, “Your son is skilled. He can work for me.”

Ethan was grateful. Sandra, however, felt a bit helpless. Wesley had just threatened Ethan with the car, and now he was using his son as leverage. Wesley was so cunning.

Since they had obtained the factory, they were about to go to work. Sandra couldn’t help but chuckle in the car. “I wonder if Debra would be furious to know that her daughter unintentionally helped you take control of this lucrative factory.”

Wesley was also in a good mood. “Hailey is preparing to give you the money officially.”

Sandra was a little surprised.

But then she realized that it was a natural result. “We should thank Angela for helping us so much.” It was just a simple competition between women. But Debra and Hailey were involved and now had to pay the price.

Because of Angela’s foolish actions, Wesley got the factory, and Sandra got the money. Angela was not their enemy! She was their lucky star!

Half past nine in the morning. Wesley’s car stopped in front of the Cooper Group. This couple was punctual for work. Although Wesley was the president, he had no power beyond the design department. Sandra was just the deputy director of the technical department. Debra had arrived early and had already informed every department. Everything was under her control.

So, she could still maintain a smile and personally welcome them to work. However, when they appeared at the door, Debra’s people returned and reported the situation to her. Learning that Ethan, who had always been unapproachable, had joined Wesley, Debra was furious. Even her pretty face was distorted with rage.

“Mrs. Debra Cooper.” Sandra greeted her joyfully. “What a coincidence.”

Debra clenched her teeth in anger. They worked in the same company, didn’t they? This greeting was clearly intended to provoke her!

She wouldn’t let them have their way. Fortunately, the technical department was under her control. Sandra, a country bumpkin, was just a rookie. She would probably be ridiculed and lose her dignity after just one morning!
